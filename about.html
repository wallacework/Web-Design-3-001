Room With A View

Consider the alternative of the garden office. Create for yourself a dedicated work space in your garden. Home yes, yet away from home life and all of its distractions.

A high-end garden office, however, requires building a space. Such a plan could be expensive and involve city planners, architects, designers contractors, and even homeowner association restrictions.

Believe me, I am all low-tech: a folding table, overhead umbrella, a portable insect screen, garden rug, laptap and a power strip, fly swatter and a liter water bottle. My space immediately becomes comfortable and functional.
